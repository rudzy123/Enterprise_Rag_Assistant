# NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 – Control Summary

## Purpose
This document provides a high-level summary of selected controls
from NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5 for internal reference and compliance alignment.

## Control Families Covered
- AC – Access Control
- IA – Identification and Authentication
- AU – Audit and Accountability
- IR – Incident Response

## Selected Controls

### AC-2 – Account Management
Organizations must manage information system accounts, including:
- Account creation
- Account modification
- Account disabling and removal

The organization defines roles responsible for account lifecycle management
and reviews accounts periodically.

### IA-5 – Authenticator Management
Authenticators (e.g., passwords, tokens, certificates) must be:
- Protected from unauthorized disclosure
- Replaced when compromised
- Managed according to defined lifecycle policies

### AU-6 – Audit Review, Analysis, and Reporting
Organizations must:
- Review audit records regularly
- Analyze events for indicators of compromise
- Report findings to designated authorities

### IR-4 – Incident Handling
Organizations must implement an incident handling capability that includes:
- Preparation
- Detection and analysis
- Containment, eradication, and recovery
- Post-incident activities

## Notes
This summary is not a replacement for the full NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 standard.
It is intended for internal guidance and AI-assisted knowledge retrieval.
