# Access Control Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines requirements for managing logical access
to organizational systems and data.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and service accounts.

## Policy Statements
- Access must follow the principle of least privilege
- Privileged access requires approval and documentation
- Access reviews must occur at least quarterly
- Access must be revoked immediately upon termination

## Responsibilities
- Managers approve access requests
- IT enforces technical controls
- Security audits access records

## Violations
Violations may result in disciplinary action or access revocation.
